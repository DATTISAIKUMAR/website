Birla Institute of Technology & Science

BITS is a private university that is highly regarded and seen as somewhat equal to the other government universities. BITS has a reputation for housing some of the best faculty members worldwide for each subject. The fantastic faculty aside, they are also very popular with students as they offer quality placements with high average packages for students in almost every stream. Let's look at their admissions process; courses offered average packages, and notable alumni.

Admission Process

  • You must complete 10+2 education from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, Maths as your compulsory subjects.
  • You have to appear for the (BITSAT) Birla Institute of Technology & Science Admission Test for being eligible for admission.
  • You can also get direct admission at BITS if you are a board topper of any recognized education board in India.

    • Courses Offered

    It should be noted that BITS does not offer BTech courses, and they offer to BE courses which they claim to be challenging science courses that focus on engineering and mathematics. You can also find BPharm and MSc courses at BITS.

    Average Package in Various Courses

    For BE courses, not BTech, the average package for students come around 18 lakhs per annum. The average package for other courses such as BPharm is about 9-11 lakhs per annum for non-core courses. For core courses, the number is lower at around 4-6 lakhs per annum.
