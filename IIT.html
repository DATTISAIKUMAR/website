Indian Institute of Technology

IIT is perhaps the most popular and prestigious engineering college in India. Only the best students from all over the country have a chance to study at IITs, and their merit had rewarded as they get fantastic job offers from some of the top companies around the world.

There are a total of 23 IITs; although all of them are not equal in terms of the platform they provide for students, all of them are equally hard to get into, at least. Let's talk about the admission process for IITs, the courses they offer, the average package you could be offered, and some of their notable alumni.

Admission Process

Getting admission to an IIT is a pretty tricky task.

  • It starts with clearing your higher secondary examination with Physics, Chemistry, Maths as your compulsory subjects.
  • Then you need to ace your JEE Mains examinations and be among the top 1,50,000 scorers on that examination to qualify for JEE Advanced.
  • You need to ace your JEE Advanced and score at least 10% in Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and at least 35% marks in aggregate.
  • You will then be eligible for an All India Rank.
  • That rank will place you among the 23 IITs present in India by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority.

    • If you want to get placed in the top IITs in the country, you have to be at the top half of the list in all the examinations listed above.

    Courses Offered

    IITs offers courses like BSc, BTech, MSc, MTech, BArch, BDes, MDes, MArch, PhD., MBA, and MPhil. These are the types of courses offered by IITs. You can find many subjects which these courses would offer. You also have to check with the guidelines of each particular IIT location to ensure they provide the specific course you are looking for or not.

    Average Package In Various Courses

    The average package for BTech courses from an IIT ranges between 12-15 lakhs per annum, and this average is for placements in domestic companies. If you get placed in foreign MNCs, you can get offers worth crores as well.
    You can expect packages that range around 10-12 lakhs per annum for corporate positions and 6-8 lakhs for research positions for other BS and other courses. For an MBA from IITs, you can expect a package of 16-25 lakhs as your starting offers. The offers can be more, but usually, it is pretty attractive.
