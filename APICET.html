Examination

Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (APICET) is a statelevel MBA and MCA entrance examination conducted for admission to colleges in the state ofAndhra Pradesh. APICET 2020 was conducted by Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupati on behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education. APICET exam is conducted for 2 hours 30 minutes in online mode. The candidates have to take the exam at their respective exam centres. The question paper comprises 200 MCQs from Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, and Communication Ability.
