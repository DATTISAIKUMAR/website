National Institute of Technology

NITs are respected and prestigious institutes. There are 31 NITs, all of which are autonomous public higher education universities. 20 of the 31 are old colleges and had been the top NITs; the 11 newly added NITs are still trying to make their mark. Let's take a look at the admission process, courses offered, average placement offers, and notable alumni of NITs.

Admission Process

  • Clear higher secondary examination with a minimum of 75% aggregate marks and Physics, Chemistry, Maths as your compulsory subjects.
  • You also have to clear the JEE Mains examination, and according to the rank you receive, you will be placed in an NIT by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority according to your All India Rank.
  • You can find the specific rank cutoffs for each NIT online.

    • Courses Offered

    NITs offer courses such as BTech, BSc, MSc, BArch, MTech, MArch, MBA, MCA, MA, Mphil. These courses have offered in almost all NITs; however, you must check the website of NIT to be sure of the courses offered by the institute.

    Average Package in Various Courses

    For BTech and engineering students, the packages range between 10-15 lakhs per annum. For BArch students, the package offered is around 5-6 lakhs per annum. For other BS and MS courses, the average package range is 5-7 lakhs per annum, and for MBA, it is 6-8 lakhs per annum.
