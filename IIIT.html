Indian Institute of Information Technology

IITs are relatively new, and not many people are aware of them. IIITs are public and private partnership universities specifically curated to technical education in India. They are currently one of the best institutes for students to pursue their BTech degrees as they are made explicitly for engineering studies.

Because of the public and private model, these institutes have a high fee, offering the highest packages. Let's take a closer look at the admission, courses offered, average packages, and notable alumni of IITs.

Admission Process

  • You must have completed (10+2) higher secondary education from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, Maths as your compulsory subjects.
  • You can get admission from your JEE Mains score.
  • You can also access the Special Channel of Admission by appearing for a different Undergraduate Entrance Examination or the Lateral Entry Entrance Exam.

    • Courses Offered

    IIITs offer BTech, MTech, Ph.D., MS, Mphil, PGSSP, and MSIT programs in various subjects. You have to go through the official website of each IIIT to ensure your choice of the study program is available at the IIIT of your choice.

    Average Package in Various Courses

    The average package for BTech at IIITs starts from 16 lakhs per annum, and you can get better offers as per your merit. The data for the average package also varies in terms of the stream of engineering, and you can find stream-specific package information online.
